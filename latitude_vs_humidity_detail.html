Comparison: City Latitude vs Humidity

- From this graph, I see that many cities in the zone of -40 to +40 latitude have high humidity.
- A humid subtropical climate is sceen in cities located between latitudes 25 - 45 degree.
- These zones of climate are usually characterized by hot and humid summers, and mild winters.
