Comparison: City Latitude vs Wind Speed

- This graph shows that wind speed is more or less uniform across the world in majority.
- Looks like city location in far northern (Latitude 60 to 80) and far southern hemisphere (latitude -2- to -40) has a tendency of high wind speed.
- Locations near the ocean like Caribbean sea, Atlantic Ocean (latitude 15.326572,) the winds are high.

Note:
To accomplish this analysis, the data is pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API, then executed over 500 random cities accross the world to generate the graph.
