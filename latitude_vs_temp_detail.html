Comparison: City Latitude vs Max Temperature

- This graph compares cities located accross the world and it shows their maximum temperature.
- This graph clearly shows that cities which are closer to zero latitude, their temperatures are on higher side.
- Cities closer to latitude zero has highest temperature.
- The temperature drops for cities which are located further North.
- Temperature tends to be on the higher side for cities which are located on southern hemisphere.

Note:
To accomplish this analysis, the data is pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API, then executed over 500 random cities accross the world to generate the graph.
