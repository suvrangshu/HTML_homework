Comparison: City Latitude vs Cloudiness


- This graph is scattered, and shows that cloudiness is scattered across all the world.
- The Sahara and Middle East are obvious dry spots, as they ae located at 23.4162° N, 25.6628° E and 29.2985° N, 42.5510° Erespectively.
- Note that deserts are common too around ±30° latitude; air descends there, inhibiting cloud formation, encouraging desertification.

Note:
To accomplish this analysis, the data is pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API, then executed over 500 random cities accross the world to generate the graph.
